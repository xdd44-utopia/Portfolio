Decisive Moment




Photography | Hong Kong

"To me, photography is the simultaneous recognition , in a fraction of a second, of the significance of an event, as well as of a precise organization of forms which give that event its proper expression."






-- Henri Cartier-Bresson --

By the term "Decisive Moment", Henri Cartier-Bresson tried to defined the action of capturing something deep and meaningful with photography. Through my wandering in streets, I also noticed such "moments". I successfully captured some of them, incorporating my own understanding, and telling the stories of Hongkongers without words.
