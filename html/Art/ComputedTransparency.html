Computed Transparency

MIT Architecture Studio Parametric Design Computational Geometry

Individual final project, 4.105 Geometric Disciplines and Architecture Skills, MIT Master of Architecture

Instructor: J. Jih

Source code on GitHub

The major part of this project mainly investigates and integrates two geometrical techniques: Computationally generated forms with trigonometric functions and arbitrary shape's curved folding of different materials with a certain thickness. Further, the final project aims at creating dynamic transparency through accurate control of parameters including strip and gap width, dynamic radius, and different materials.
