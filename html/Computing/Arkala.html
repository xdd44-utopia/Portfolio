Arkala: LLM-Driven Game

Game Design Large Language Model Role-Playing Game

Over the summer of 2024, Ben, my undergrad game developer mate, and I collaborated again to explore the potential of large language model on developing narrative games.

Project Description

Arkala is a role-playing game empowered by AI, where generative agents breathe life into a dynamic world reminiscent of Westworld. Every NPC has their own memory, goals, and motivations, creating an ever-evolving narrative landscape where no two playthroughs are the same.

Meaningful interactions lie at the heart of Arkala. Player choices ripple through the world, shaping relationships and unlocking dynamic narratives that respond to every decision made throughout the journey.

By harnessing the power of large language models and generative agents, Arkala pushes the boundaries of traditional RPGs, offering an experience where the story is truly shaped by the player.
