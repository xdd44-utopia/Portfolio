Greetings from an Internet-addicted boy!

Hello! My name is Chenyue DAI and I always use "xdd44" as a nickname online.

I'm currently studying Bachelor degree in Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong. But actually my dream is to become an architect.

I'd like to say that there're so many things in the world I deeply love to do. I love learning Computer Science at my university and coding day to night. I love exploring lines and shapes and even cultures through my extracurricular study in architecture. I love taking photos trying to show the beauty of objects, streets, nature and people through my camera. Sometimes I may have a try on painting or video editing... There's never been enough time for me to spend.

The free university life allows me to devote myself to all these things I love, where I made many tries and realized many ideas. About my logo

As you may have already noticed, there's a triangle in the icon at top left of this website (mouse over to zoom it) and also in favicon/website icon. This triangle is what I always used as my logo.

The design of this logo began when I was doing some painting in junior high. One day I came up with the idea that I need a logo as a signature for these paints and this triangle shape just came out of nowhere.

I thought this triangle is pretty good with color of ballpen blue I used, so I decided to use it gave it a geometrical definition. I'd like to underline that this pattern is truly meaningless. I always think it's unnecessary to find explanations for everything in life. Things may pop up for no reason and I'm glad to accept them as inspirations.
