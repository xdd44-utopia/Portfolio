MacroHK

Unlike MicroHK, photos here are just normal copies of what our eyes see, tourist photos in another word. But I still want to gather these photos because there's an interesting fact: There's no end of being a tourist in Hong Kong. I've lived here for two years and kept going out for photography, but I can still find something new and beatiful to shoot after those thousands of shutters and I'm confident that I can continue to be a tourist in Hong Kong for many more years. Unfortunately, I'm here just for a four-year university life, so I'm going to take photos as much as I can before I say goodbye to this amazing city.
