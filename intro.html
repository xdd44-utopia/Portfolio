If you are curious about my logo...


As you may have already noticed, there's a triangle in the icon at top left of this website (mouse over to zoom it) and also in favicon/website icon. This triangle is what I always used as my logo.


The design of this logo began when I was doing some painting in junior high. One day I came up with the idea that I need a logo as a signature for these paints and this triangle shape just came out of nowhere.


I thought this triangle is pretty good with color of ballpen blue I used, so I decided to gave it a geometrical definition. I'd like to underline that this pattern is truly meaningless. I always think it's unnecessary to find explanations for everything in life. Things may pop up for no reason and I'm glad to accept them as inspirations.


Years passed until I finally struggled through the heavy Senior High life. After NCEE I thoght I can combine this pattern with visualized version of my nickname "xdd44". I used lines and curves to draw these characters and after lots of adjustments by Ai the result became like this.


I tried to make it as simple as possible so lines of different characters are connected. After all I still thought it is too wide and complex for either logo or profile photo or watermark so I seldom use it. Thus, a new design was needed. When I looked at the last design I suddenly found that I can use lines to make the triangle more soft. Eventually, the line version of my logo came out.


And now I can make different modification for different occasions with this line version. Nowadays, I love to use it everywhere much more than design another handwrite signature as graphic pattern is always simple and clear and easier to make an impression.
