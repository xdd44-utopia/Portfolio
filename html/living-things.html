Living things

Strangers, an indispensable part of the streets, live their lives same as us. They are another kind of scenery, more like drama: may not be so attractive as mountains and oceans, but rather beautiful when we empathize.

The title "Living things" is the title of the 5th album of my favorite band Linkin Park. We are truly some kinds of "things" in this world. In the planet's eyes, you and me and everyone are the same dust. But we found ourselves living when we see, when we hear and when we get and share our emotions in fleeting moments.
